About Us

Approved Workmen Are Not Ashamed

AWANA is an acronym for the statement "Approved Workmen Are Not Ashamed" inspired by the Bible Verse found in 2 Timothy 2:15, which states, " Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." This ministry was adapted by Baras Happy Church early 2018. The ministry, then, stopped due to lack of manpower and issues revolving around the community. Baras Youth decided to rebuilt the ministry again, from scratch, last April 2022.
